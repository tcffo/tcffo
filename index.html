Education

restoring possibilities and hope for youth and families

We are a non-profit agency that offers education, intervention and support for families
who are challenged by the adolescent years. Our programs and support systems
help youth and families sustain long term, positive changes within the family unit.

volunteers and interns needed

The Center for Family Outreach relies on unpaid interns and volunteer support to help make our organization a success. Our greatest need for volunteers includes the new mentoring program, event coordination, fundraising, and administrative office duties. Other opportunities for part-time volunteering are listed below.

Internship Opportunities

Youth Programs Intern

Volunteers are needed to teach yoga, dance, voice lessons, cooking, painting, pottery, drawing, technology, bike building, photography, and weightlifting.

Resource Development Intern

This is an excellent internship opportunity for any student who hopes to pursue a career in fundraising for non-profits or customer relationship management.

Project Management Intern

We are looking for someone to help plan monthly dinners for youth and their families.

Research/Writing Intern

We need a volunteer who can write and research grants and would like to edit and write our monthly newsletter.

Communications Intern

We need volunteers to help greet clients, answer and direct phone calls, and organize resources.

Online Marketing Intern

The Center for Family Outreach is seeking a volunteer who would be interested in maintaining our social media accounts.

Volunteer Opportunities

Tutor

Are you someone who loves to help students with homework? We have students who need your assistance! Currently needing Math, French, and German tutors!

Child Care

Child care providers are needed during our "Families Working Together for Success" dinner and family therapy sessions.

Life Skills

We are looking for some energetic people who would like to teach life skills including cooking, nutrition, grocery shopping, budgeting, etc.

City Transportation

We need someone who knows how the public transportation systems in Fort Collins and/or Loveland work and can instruct clients and their families.
