Education

restoring possibilities and hope for youth and families

We are a non-profit agency that offers education, intervention and support for families
who are challenged by the adolescent years. Our programs and support systems
help youth and families sustain long term, positive changes within the family unit.

volunteers needed

The Center for Family Outreach relies on volunteer support to help make our organization a success. Our greatest need for volunteers includes the new mentoring program, event coordination, fundraising, and administrative office duties. Other opportunities for volunteering are listed below.

volunteer opportunities

Mentor

Volunteers are needed to mentor students and inspire positive youth development in the lives of at-risk youth (e.g. Homework help, goal setting, pro-social activities, etc.).

Receptionist

We need a volunteer who would be willing to provide administrative assistance at the front desk, greeting clients, answering and directing phone calls, organizing files and resources.

Babysitter

Child care providers are needed during the monthly "Families Working Together for Success" dinner and family therapy sessions.

Writer

The Center for Family Outreach is seeking a volunteer who loves to write. We need an individual who can write and research grants and would like to edit and write our monthly newsletter.

Activities Coordinator

We are looking for some energetic people to coordinate some fun activities within the community for our youth this summer.

City Transportation

We need an individual who would be interested in teaching families how the public transportation systems in Fort Collins and/or Loveland work and can instruct students and their families.

Help teach

Tutor

Are you someone who loves to help students with homework? We have students who need your assistance! Currently needing Math, French, and German tutors!

Activities

We are looking for those interested in teaching yoga, dance, voice lessons, cooking, painting, pottery, drawing, sound engineering, bike building, photography, weightlifting, etc.

Life Skills

We are looking for some energetic people to teach hands on life skills classes. Current interests include cooking, nutrition, grocery shopping, budgeting, etc.

Public Transportation

We need someone who knows how the public transportation systems in Fort Collins and/or Loveland work and can instruct clients and their families.
